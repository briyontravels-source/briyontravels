02SIGIRIYA TOUR
details

Main Destination

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress located in the central part of Sri Lanka. It was built in the 5th century by King Kashyapa and is famous for its impressive architecture, beautiful frescoes, and advanced garden design. The site features the iconic Lion Gate, the Mirror Wall, and well-planned water gardens at the base of the rock. Sigiriya is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is considered one of the most important historical and cultural landmarks in Sri Lanka.
