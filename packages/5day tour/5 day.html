01GALLE FORT
details

Main Destination

The main destination of this journey is Galle Fort, a historic coastal fortress renowned for its rich heritage, colonial architecture, and stunning ocean views.

Journey Route

The roads are well maintained, allowing you to travel comfortably in our air-conditioned vehicle.

Stop Places

  • Sea Turtle Hatchery (Hikkaduwa / Kosgoda)
  • River Safari (Madu Ganga)

“Throughout the tour, you are free to visit places like these based on your interests.”

Food & Activities

  • Cafe 44 / Pedlar’s Inn / Isle of Gelato
  • Rooftop cafes

“Accompanied by our professional guide, guests may stop at selected locations like these for meals, refreshments, and relaxation breaks throughout the tour. These are provided as examples only.”

Final Experience

Guests will be safely dropped off at either our reserved hotel or their preferred hotel.
